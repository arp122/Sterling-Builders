Coming up on Hoshangabad Road, Pacific Business Centre is a reflection of the changing aspirations of retailers and investors seeking commercial space with low operational costs, high visibility and affluent catchment area. The centre is designed to render a high street appeal and offer fabulous connectivity for commercial and retail ventures to flourish.

Pacific Business Center has been designed to emerge as an ultimate commercial landmark in Bhopal. In nutshell a well integrated destination catering to local and tourists as well.

Amenities

Business Centre

From architectural design to offering world class amenities and infrastructure, one thing has been given prime importance, functionality and efficiency. Access to office floors is through high speed elevators. Fundamentals of passive solar architecture has been imbibed to make it an energy efficient building. The electronic systems, hardware and software platforms have been completely coupled to provide a highly efficient and intelligent building with an integrated building management system. Overlooking a magnificently landscaped courtyard, these modern offices will increase efficiency and output of your enterprise manifold.

  • High visibility of showrooms from common areas
  • High frontage for showrooms

  • Magnificent atrium with multi level view

Shoppers Paradise

The ground, first and second floors of the Pacific Business Centre offer an open and vibrant arena with large floor plates for plates for bigger showrooms, connected by wide corridors and elevators. Arranged around a spacious atrium, each showroom has excellent visibility from the atrium or from the exterior. This fully integrated shopping centre will include every modern facility and would make your every trip to it fully enjoyable. Adequate signages, wide corridors, sufficient car parking bays, are just our ways of ensuring that shopping sprees of customers are truly stress and hassle free. Shoppers Paradise includes - Departmental stores, Hyper Market, Anchor Stores, Electronics, Furniture and Automobile Showrooms.

Hotel Space

The luxury hotel is indeed going to be a modern marvel that would further augment the idea of "staying close to vivacity without compromising on privacy". The hotel will be a 60-90 room luxury complete with a convention hall and rooms that define comfort and privilege. The beautifully designed hotel will be constructed in conformity with international standards. With an independent entrance, lift lounge and reception on the ground floor, the hotel assures of complete privacy to its customers. The hotel with retail zone and corporate offices is the finest solution for business travelers as well as tourists.

  • Wi-fi enabled complex
  • Convention and banqueting facilities
  • Separate entrance for Hotel with dedicated car parking
  • Lift lounge and reception on ground floor
  • Fitness club for hotel guests and members

Food 'n' Fun

Pacific Business Centre will not only be known for corporate offices but also as an entertainment hub that would consist of a thrilling shopping precinct coupled with tempting eating options, fine dining restaurants, food court and a multiplex.

Two Screen Multiplex - Experience the entrancing fantasy of movie-dom in a state-of-the-art multiplex that will be encumbered with sophisticated technology, plush seating, extra-dimensional screens, sophisticated dolby digital sound system and a vibrant ambience.

Play Zone for Kids - A dedicated play zone is being envisaged within the precincts. The zone shall comprise of gratifying atmosphere with a video gaming arcade and fun rides to keep the whole family busy.

Food Court - The multi-cuisine food court is designed to pamper taste buds with its exclusive cuisines. This eating-zone is going to be quite different from the traditional food court and will be designed like a luxurious lounge. The zone also has fine dining restaurants and night clubs.
